Qaboli Palaw

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb lamb
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 onion, slided
  • Spices: cardamom, cinnamon, cumin

Instructions:

  1. Soak the rice for 30 minutes an boil until 80% cooked.
  2. Fry onions and lamb with spices.
  3. Layer meat, rice, carrots, and raisins in a pot.
  4. Steam for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with naan or yogurt.
